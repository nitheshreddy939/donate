Welcome to Donate for Hopeless

We are here to help everyone in need

We are providing children of today (who are the future of tomorrow) theright tools and paving the way for them to march ahead in life. The organisation is helping deprived children get a chance at a fulfilling childhood by providing them quality learning opportunities, access to healthcare, protection from abuse and harm and relief and rehabilitation during disasters and emergencies. The idea is to empower the most marginalised children of India to grow up into mature, healthy and skilled adults who can earn a decent livelihood and leave behind the life of poverty.
